How to Use These Worksheets

These worksheets are designed for use by licensed clinicians during sessions or as between-session homework. Here are a few tips for getting the most out of them.

Introduce During Session

Walk through each worksheet collaboratively with the client the first time. Model how to complete it, explain the rationale, and answer questions before assigning it as homework.

Review Consistently

Begin each session by reviewing completed worksheets. This reinforces the skill, shows the client you value their effort, and provides rich clinical material for discussion.

Adapt to Your Client

These are starting points, not rigid protocols. Modify language for developmental level, cultural context, and reading ability. Consider translating key terms for multilingual clients.
