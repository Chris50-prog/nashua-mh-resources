Clinical Documentation

Documentation Templates

Step-by-step clinical note builders for SOAP, DAP, case conferences, group sessions, and collateral contacts. Complete each section and export a formatted note for your EHR.

No data is stored or transmitted — everything stays in your browser
SOAP Note
Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan. The standard progress note format for individual therapy sessions.
7 steps ~5 min
DAP Note
Data, Assessment, Plan. A concise alternative to SOAP for streamlined session documentation.
5 steps ~3 min
Case Conference
Multi-disciplinary team meeting documentation with discussion points, recommendations, and action items.
5 steps ~4 min
Group Therapy
Group session notes with per-member tracking, therapeutic factors, and group process documentation.
6 steps ~6 min
Collateral Contact
External contact documentation including phone calls, emails, coordination with providers, schools, and agencies.
4 steps ~2 min
