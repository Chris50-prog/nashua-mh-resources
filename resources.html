Greater Nashua Mental Health Center (GNMHC)

(603) 889-6147
330 Main St, Nashua, NH 03060 | gnmhc.org | Mon-Fri 8 AM - 5 PM; crisis 24/7
Designated Community Mental Health Center for Greater Nashua. Outpatient individual/group/family therapy, psychiatric evaluation, medication management, Community Support Program (CSP), ACT team, crisis services, supported employment/education, dual-disorder treatment, child/adolescent services, school-based clinicians, geriatric MH, housing support, Court Diversion, trauma-focused therapies (EMDR, CPT, PE), DBT skills groups.
CMHC Sliding Scale All Ages Medicaid SMI Priority

How to Refer (Step-by-Step)

▼

Eligibility: All Greater Nashua residents. Priority for SMI adults and SED children. No one turned away for inability to pay.

Insurance: Medicaid (all NH MCOs), Medicare, most private insurance, sliding-scale for uninsured.

Call (603) 889-6147 and ask for Intake/Access
Provide client name, DOB, insurance info, and brief clinical summary
Client scheduled for intake assessment (typically 1-2 weeks; faster for urgent needs)
Intake assessment determines level of care and assigns treatment team
For urgent needs, request expedited intake or crisis services

Clinician note: GNMHC is mandated to serve individuals with SMI and cannot refuse service to this population.

Benefits / Financial Assistance

SSI (Supplemental Security Income)

1-800-772-1213
SSA, 70 Commercial St, Nashua | ssa.gov
Monthly cash benefit (~$943/month, 2026) for aged, blind, or disabled with limited income/resources. NH has no state supplement. Decision in 3-6+ months. If denied, appeal within 60 days.
SSI Disability Cash Benefit

How to Apply

▼
Call SSA at 1-800-772-1213 or visit office (appointment recommended)
Complete application (can start online at ssa.gov)
Provide medical records documenting disability
SSA sends to Disability Determination Services for review
Decision in 3-6 months; if denied, appeal within 60 days

Clinician note: Provide detailed functional assessment letters focusing on how disability limits ability to work, not just diagnosis. Consider disability attorney referral.

SSDI (Social Security Disability Insurance)

1-800-772-1213
SSA, 70 Commercial St, Nashua | ssa.gov
Monthly benefit (avg ~$1,500/month) for workers who can no longer work due to disability. 5-month waiting period after approval. Medicare eligibility 24 months after entitlement. Can apply for both SSI and SSDI simultaneously.
SSDI Disability Work History
