Brain-Body Connection

Stress Response (HPA Axis)
When you feel stressed, your brain sends a signal that releases the stress hormone cortisol. This affects your whole body.
🧠
Hypothalamus detects threat
↓
⚛
Releases CRH → Pituitary releases ACTH
↓
💧
Adrenal glands release cortisol
↓
❤
Heart rate, blood pressure, digestion, immunity, sleep all affected
Anxiety & the Nervous System
When your brain's alarm center (amygdala) fires too often, it triggers your body's fight-or-flight response, causing the physical symptoms of anxiety.
🚨
Amygdala sounds the alarm
↓
⚡
Sympathetic nervous system activates
↓
💓
Racing heart, rapid breathing, sweating
↓
🫠
Muscle tension, stomach upset, dizziness
Depression & the Body
When brain chemicals like serotonin and dopamine are out of balance, it doesn't just affect your mood. It affects your energy, sleep, appetite, and even your ability to feel pleasure.
🔬
Serotonin & dopamine levels drop
↓
💤
Sleep disrupted (too much or too little)
↓
🍕
Appetite changes (increase or decrease)
↓
😢
Low energy, loss of motivation and pleasure
