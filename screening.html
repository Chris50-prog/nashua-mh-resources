Clinical Instruments

Screening Tools

Standardized, validated screening instruments for depression, anxiety, substance use, and suicide risk -- administered one question at a time.

PHQ-9
Patient Health Questionnaire
Screens for depression severity. 9 questions about mood, energy, sleep, appetite, and self-harm over the past 2 weeks.
9 questions ~2 min
GAD-7
Generalized Anxiety Disorder Scale
Screens for anxiety severity. 7 questions about nervousness, worry, restlessness, and fear over the past 2 weeks.
7 questions ~2 min
AUDIT
Alcohol Use Disorders Identification Test
Screens for hazardous and harmful alcohol use. 10 questions about drinking frequency, quantity, and consequences.
10 questions ~3 min
DAST-10
Drug Abuse Screening Test
Screens for drug use problems. 10 yes/no questions about drug-related consequences over the past 12 months (excluding alcohol and tobacco).
10 questions ~2 min
C-SSRS
Columbia Suicide Severity Rating Scale
Triage screening for suicide risk. 6 yes/no questions assessing ideation, planning, intent, and behavior. Determines risk level.
6 questions ~1 min
PCL-5
PTSD Checklist for DSM-5
Screens for PTSD symptom severity. 20 questions about how much you were bothered by trauma-related symptoms over the past month.
20 questions ~5 min
WHODAS 2.0
WHO Disability Assessment Schedule
Measures functioning and disability across six life domains. 12-item short form covering the past 30 days.
12 questions ~3 min
K10
Kessler Psychological Distress Scale
Screens for non-specific psychological distress. 10 questions about anxiety and depressive feelings over the past 30 days.
10 questions ~3 min
CAGE-AID
CAGE Adapted to Include Drugs
Brief screen for alcohol and drug problems. 4 yes/no questions. A score of 2 or more is clinically significant.
4 questions ~1 min
EPDS
Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale
Screens for perinatal depression and anxiety. 10 questions about feelings over the past 7 days, including a self-harm item.
10 questions ~3 min
Zarit (Short)
Zarit Burden Interview — 12-Item
Measures caregiver burden. 12 questions about the strain of caring for a person with illness or disability.
12 questions ~3 min
CD-RISC-10
Connor-Davidson Resilience Scale
Measures psychological resilience. 10 statements about adapting to change and bouncing back. Higher scores indicate more resilience.
10 questions ~3 min
ORS
Outcome Rating Scale
Brief session-by-session measure of well-being across four areas. Rate each 0–10 looking back over the past week.
4 items ~1 min
SRS
Session Rating Scale
Measures the therapeutic alliance at the end of a session. Rate the relationship, goals, approach, and overall fit 0–10.
4 items ~1 min
WEMWBS
Warwick-Edinburgh Mental Well-Being Scale
Measures positive mental well-being. 14 statements about feelings and thoughts over the past 2 weeks. Higher scores indicate greater well-being.
14 questions ~4 min
