Quick Triage Guide

What's the situation? ▼ Collapse
Client in psychiatric crisis
Call NH Rapid Response 1-833-710-6477 for MCRT dispatch. Immediate danger? Call 911. In ED? Request ACCESS assessment.
Client needs MH treatment
Call GNMHC (603) 889-6147 for intake. PHP/IOP: Discover Health Group (603) 402-7551.
Client needs SUD treatment now
Call NH Doorway (603) 836-6540 for same-day assessment and bed placement.
Client is homeless tonight
Call 2-1-1 for coordinated entry. Nashua Soup Kitchen (603) 889-7770. DV? Bridges (603) 883-3044.
Client needs food today
Nashua Soup Kitchen, 2 Quincy St — daily meals. SNAP: nheasy.nh.gov or DHHS 137 Temple St.
Client is uninsured
Harbor Care (603) 882-3616 (FQHC, sliding-scale). Insurance: nheasy.nh.gov.
Client is undocumented
Harbor Care serves all regardless of status. Catholic Charities (603) 669-3030 for immigration legal.
Client needs SSI/SSDI
SSA 1-800-772-1213 or 70 Commercial St, Nashua. Provide functional assessment letter.
Client is a DV survivor
Bridges crisis line (603) 883-3044, 24/7. Shelter, safety planning, legal advocacy.
Client is a veteran
Manchester VA (603) 624-4366. Housing: Harbor Care. Crisis: 988, press 1.
Elderly client needs services
ServiceLink (603) 889-6220 — aging and disability services, benefits, care coordination.
Don't know where to start?
Call 2-1-1. Available 24/7 — specialists search the entire NH resource database.
