Quick Triage Guide

What's the situation?
Client in psychiatric crisis
Call NH Rapid Response 1-833-710-6477 for MCRT dispatch. Immediate danger? Call 911. Already in ED? Request ACCESS psychiatric assessment.
Client needs MH treatment
Call GNMHC (603) 889-6147 for intake. For PHP/IOP: Discover Health Group (603) 402-7551 or Southern NH Health.
Client needs SUD treatment now
Call NH Doorway (603) 836-6540 for same-day assessment and bed placement. They access the statewide treatment bed board.
Client is homeless tonight
Call 2-1-1 for coordinated entry. Direct to Nashua Soup Kitchen & Shelter (603) 889-7770. DV? Bridges (603) 883-3044.
Client needs food today
Nashua Soup Kitchen & Shelter, 2 Quincy St — daily meals, no questions asked. SNAP: apply at nheasy.nh.gov or DHHS 137 Temple St.
Client is uninsured
Harbor Care (603) 882-3616 for medical/dental/BH (FQHC, sliding-scale). Insurance: apply at nheasy.nh.gov or visit DHHS.
Client is undocumented
Harbor Care serves everyone regardless of status. Catholic Charities (603) 669-3030 for immigration legal help.
Client needs SSI/SSDI
SSA 1-800-772-1213 or visit 70 Commercial St, Nashua. Provide detailed functional assessment letter. Consider disability attorney if denied.
Client is a DV survivor
Bridges crisis line (603) 883-3044, 24/7. Coordinates shelter, safety planning, legal advocacy, and all related needs.
Client is a veteran
Manchester VA (603) 624-4366. Housing: Harbor Care veteran programs. Crisis: 988, press 1.
Elderly client needs services
ServiceLink (603) 889-6220 — one-stop shop for all aging and disability services, benefits, and care coordination.
Don't know where to start?
Call 2-1-1. Available 24/7, specialists search the entire NH resource database. They'll connect you to the right place.
